## How to Apply

1. Pick a palette above.
2. Map its values into your theme config (`themeConfig`, `AGENT_RING_COLORS`, CSS variables).
3. Update `--mesh-accent`, `--orb-*`, and `.agent-status-ring.*` in `App.css`.
4. Optionally add a palette selector (e.g. in the toolbar) to switch between presets.
